Privacy & Cookie policy

 

The protection of your privacy and the security of your personal data are very important to MiWi B.V. (hereafter "MIWI"), which is the company responsible for the “Vurze application. We handle personal data in accordance with the provisions of the Dutch Personal Data Protection Act (Wet bescherming persoonsgegevens) as well as the provisions of other laws, regulations and rules, which are applicable to Vurze.

We refer to your "privacy settings" where you can arrange who can see your profile, interests and favourites. You can also choose in such settings whether your friends can share posts you share with them, the frequency of activity mails we send you (weekly, daily, or none) and if you want to stop receiving news about Vurze.

MIWI may amend this Privacy & Cookie policy from time to time (date of last amendment: 1-12-2015). On this website or application, you can always view the latest version of our Privacy & Cookie policy. If we make changes to the Privacy & Cookie policy we will provide notice on the website and application or inform you so that you may review the changes before you continue to use the application respectively application. If you object to such changes, you have to delete your account. Continuing to use the application or service after such a notice has been published or communicated means that you are consenting to the changes.

This application is intended for users who are sixteen years or older. If you are younger, you should ask your parents or legal representatives, for permission before you register.

What do we use your data for?

1. We will process your personal data in connection with the provision of our services.

This website allows users to add private and/or public: posts, interests and share information about interests, experiences or activities with others.  

You can register an account either by logging into the website using your Google account (we will then receive your user ID, name, birth date, email address, gender and likes) or by using your email address. When choosing the latter we will ask for a username and a password.

When registering you can add interests. In addition, you can create a profile on the website and for example include information about your age, where you live, your picture and interests. We will also send you activity mails and you can change the frequency thereof in your privacy settings (weekly, daily, or none).

If you have shared a post, please be aware that such content can also be shared on other websites or applications on the internet. If you share content with buddies or followers, please note that they have the option to share your post, unless you change this in your privacy settings.

When you post and decide to share it, it means that everyone who has a registered account on Vurze can access and use that content and associate it with you.

We may edit and make formatting changes to your content (such as translating it and modifying the size).

Please do not share personal data of others unless you have that person's permission.

2. If you send us an email, we will use your personal data for the purpose of responding to your questions or comments.

3. In the context of our relationship with you, we may also send you newsletters about Vurze and/or other products and services by e-mail, message or notification.

We can also send you notices about your account even if you have opted to no longer receive news from Vurze.

4. We also analyse your use of the application, your profile (such as age, gender and place of residence), activities, your posts, interests, as well as your behaviour which allows us to improve the website and/or service.

We have the right to show you relevant, promoted content from buddies, people or companies you follow. These promoted posts will not be displayed when you stop following the source of the post. We offer this service to allow companies or users to enlarge their organic reach. Vurze will appropriately mark these if and when used.

We will not include your content in advertisements for the products and services for others to others.

We also collect general data concerning visitors and therefore process IP addresses, as well as information concerning screen resolution, the browser type and the behaviour of users in Vurze, our website and application. This for example entails the number of visitors and the number of clicks on certain pages. These data are used to improve our services, as well our website or application and to adapt these to your personal settings, preferences and/or personal situation. "Cookies" are used (for more information see hereafter). If you access the website or application through your mobile device we save your IP address (except the last three numbers of the IP address which are removed before the IP address is stored), browser- and device type through Google Analytics (for more information on the Google Analytics cookies used see hereafter). Google Analytics also collects your location data (IP address of your PC or the location coordinates of your device). We also sometimes record (a 10% sample of all users) video of users screen, ONLY within the app, when its open and the screen is on, and not any notifications from third party apps that are visible while the app is open via Appsee. We track crashes, session duration, type of device, OS version, connectivity type, device resolution. Next to Google Analytics we use Mixpanel to anonymously track events connected with UI elements at the individual level.

5. We may process your personal data for the purpose of complying with a statutory requirement.

6. We may provide your personal data to third parties if there is a claim of such a third party which we feel outweighs your right to privacy for example in case of illegal activities or slander, or keep the relevant personal data for such purposes.

7. We also process your personal data for internal purposes such as ensuring security of the application, Vurze, yourself or others, detect, prevent and combat fraud or other illegal activities, problem solving, testing and improvement of service. We may also review content or created interests to determine whether it is illegal, breaches applicable sanction regulations or violates our policies or any agreement with you and in that regard we may remove or refuse to display content that we deem inappropriate.

There is also a "Report" button, behind the more button (three vertical dots) on the right top of every post which enables other users to report any content or activity on our service which the user believes to be in violation with the standards included in the Terms of Use. This can also be reported by users sending an email to legal@miwi.com.

8. We may disclose your personal data to a third party further to a sale of the assets of MIWI or as the result of a change in control of MIWI or in preparation for any of these events to the extent permitted by applicable law.

Your personal data may be processed by us and/or our service providers on our behalf for the purposes specified above. If such country does not offer a level of personal data protection similar to the European Union, we will ensure there are adequate safeguards to protect your personal data.

Cookies

This application uses cookies. Cookies are small files that are placed on the user's computer when he/she visits a website. We use session cookies to enable your visit for the duration of your session, as well as cookies for analytics and logging in. Our login cookies have a life span of 24 hours, unless you opt-in for us to remember your login details, then the cookie has a lifespan of a week. Every time you login with us remembering your login details the lifespan of the cookies is prolonged by a week.

In addition we use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. In this regard, our website will be analysed in order to, for example, count the number of visitors to the site and see which pages are visited the most. This will enable us to improve our website and our services. Google Analytics uses cookies to carry out its analysis. These cookies are (currently) stored for a period ranging from the duration of the session to 2 years. Data which are recorded include your IP address (except the last three numbers of the IP address which are removed before the IP address is stored by Google Analytics), the pages visited, the time and duration of the visit, your location, the origin of the visit, the internet provider, your browser & device type used and the interaction with the application. The data obtained will be transferred to Google and saved by Google on servers in the United States. To learn more about Google Analytics you could click on the following link: http://www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html.

We have entered into a data processing agreement with Google as a data processor with respect to your data. We do not share your personal data with Google for their own purposes. We do not use other Google services in combination with Google Analytics cookies.

We use Mixpanel, a service provided by MIXPANEL, INC., an analytics service that tracks user behavior to optimize our user experience. Mixpanel uses cookies to carry out its analysis. To find more about Mixpanel’s click here: https://mixpanel.com/privacy/.

We also use Appsee, an analytics service provided by Shift 6 Ltd., to see how users behave on the application and gain insights into better serving them. This services uses cookies to carry out its analysis. To find out more about Appsee click here: https://www.appsee.com/legal/privacypolicy.

Most internet browsers can be set or re-set to accept cookies, block cookies or notify you whenever you receive a cookie. To find out how you can change your browser settings and remove cookies, see the instructions for the relevant browser and/or use the "Help" function (this can usually be accessed from the browser's toolbar). There is an opt-out possibility for Google Analytics cookies on the website which can be enforced by clicking here. [Your opt-out is registered with a cookie.]

Your rights

Please address any such requests to: info@miwi.com. If you are younger than 16 years, such requests should be done by your parent (or legal representative). Security of your data

We want your personal data to remain as secure as possible and accordingly strive to provide secure data transmission between your computer and our servers. We have taken appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against loss or against any form of unlawful processing.

Storage period

You can always delete your account. In that case Vurze will immediately remove your personal data, unless there is a statutory retention period or if it is otherwise necessary to keep personal data and to the extent permitted by applicable law for example in case of disputes, or to combat fraud and other criminal activities. Removed content may persist in backups for a limited period of time (not available to others). The foregoing also applies to messages which you have sent to others.

If you choose to deactivate your account, your account information and posts are stored and still visible for other users. If you login with a deactivated account you go to the screen straight away where you can re-activate your account.

Please be aware that if you delete your app without deleting your account, your account still exists until you delete the account as well.

If a user passes away, the user's heir can contact MIWI and we will verify the information and thereafter grant access to the account.
